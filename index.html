What is Competitive Programming?

And why should you consider doing it?

Competitive Programming is a mind sport usually held over the Internet or a Local Network where participants are given a particular problem and they have to come up with the most optimal solution.

In Competitions you must solve problems in stressful situations and do it up against a deadline or you lose. Taking part in Competitive Programming teaches you how to be more focused on the task and not only complete it quickly, but accurately. These skills are highly beneficial for any job, not just coding.

As you get used to solving harder and harder problems in contests, you will easily be able to answer questions asked in technical interviews, which increases your chances in cracking that interview.

Read

First step is to Read the question Carefully. Good questions always have a small fact/clue laid in fine print that can make the difference between solving the question and tanking it.

Think

Thinking is the most imortant thing in Competitive Programming, even more so than Coding.Take a pen and paper and think abot the best and most optimal way to approach the question.

Code

Now comes the third step.Convert your thought process into code.Try to pass all the test cases and debug. If that's not enough then go back to step two.

Compete

Solve More and more questions, Participate in more and more contests to improve your Skills. You won't even realize when you get better at Competitive Programming.
